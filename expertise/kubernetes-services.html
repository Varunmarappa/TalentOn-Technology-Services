Technology Services

A company that believes in delivering high output with qualitative results needs to augment itself with the right kind of technology.

We offer a range of cloud-based services that promise to improve organizational performance, simplify scalability, and ensures high output with optimal utilization of resources.

Public Cloud Services

Public cloud is ideal for businesses that are looking at scaling their business and leverage technology without having to own their own cloud space. Having a public cloud space eliminates the need of having your very own hardware, maintenance and managing costs. Additionally, we eliminate the need to use your own set of resources in your cloud management.

Private Cloud

Depending upon the size and needs of your organizations, some firms look towards developing their own cloud infrastructure. We help firms secure their very own infrastructure using our private cloud services. If you are looking for a higher level of security within your organizational cloud structure, this is the ideal choice. We help in the end-to-end deployment of private cloud services for your organization. It becomes imperative for the organization to manage its own maintenance, staffing, and management solutions within this type.

Hybrid Cloud

This type of cloud service allows you to share and manage between both types of cloud management services. This type of service is ideal for firms looking at short term results without investing a large number of resources in maintenance and staffing. It also allows you to gradually build a system that is safer and more efficient for larger organizations. It offers flexibility and scalability for your firm.

Dev Ops

TalentOn Technologies specialises in providing DevOps services, a systematically harmonized system that allows us to create a seamless system that increases your organizational performance and overall productivity. With DevOps, you can expect a smooth flow of systems and practices within the organization that are all in sync with one another. This includes setting up a range of processes, tools and software that ensures maximum benefit to the organization. DevOps helps your organization by improving its scalability, ensures higher reliability. We ensure that we can help improve your inter-organizational collaboration and output. Security remains our highest priority when its implementation of DevOps.

Server Management

We understand that companies heavily depend upon servers in order to meet their technological needs and organizational demands. Not every organization can afford to take up the costs of having its own servers and hiring a team of its management. We help organizations by handling their end-to-end server management services for them. With our server management services, we aim to eliminate the risk of server downtimes, build secure server environments while consistently meeting your organizational needs

Data Centre Management

With our data centre services, we ensure that our clients experience the best of our hosting and co-locating services. We understand that you may wish to outsource data centre management services owing to the complexity in management. Therefore, we come to you with the right blend of solutions that allows you to have complete storage transformation services, creating a secure and energy effective infrastructure and more. We are here to set up your entire infrastructure based on your needs, capacity and business outcome.

Software Development

We take pride in offering SaaS (Software as a service). Our team of tech experts is experienced in creating software systems that simplify your business process and improve organizational efficiency. From logistics to healthcare to financial services, we are experts in creating specialized software just for your niche. Our process consists of finding out our client requirements, creating a customized plan based on client objectives and delivering the right software for your firm. In addition, we deliver complete software deployment services and training required to adapt to the new software within minimal downtime.

Test Automation

When it comes to incorporating a new technology that simplifies your business, in reality, it's only half the job done. It is essential that these services are secure and safe in every way possible. With an increase in the adaption of new technology, the instances of cybercrimes have also significantly increased. Therefore, our TalentOn team works towards ensuring that we conducted a battery of tests that are indicators of your cybersafety. We include tests such as Pen tests, System testing, Acceptance testing, Performance testing and Security testing. We aim to weed out the potential loopholes that may occur and eliminate these vulnerabilities beforehand.

Networking & Security Solutions

As a part of our technology deployment, we also provide cybersecurity services to our clients. We create a customised cyber security plan for your organisation based on the complexity and the specific type of vulnerabilities that we expect. We create the SOPs, access the critical control points within your system, create a disaster recovery plan and ensure seamless integration into your technological process.

Learning & Development Services

Our team at TalentOn technologies has created an immersive learning & development module that enables and empowers individuals to create a strong roadmap for a secure future. We cover fundamentals of analysis, facilitation skills and others in addition to teaching specialized skills as required. Our team of experienced professionals ensure that we facilitate your growth and broaden your horizon. Thus, ensuring a more holistic growth approach, thus adding more flair to your corporate image.
