TalentON Technology Services, established in 2021, is a staffing, consultancy and managing technical projects firm in India. It offers simple and cost-effective consultancy to its clients and has a rich experience in industries such as technology, knowledge services, banking, financial services, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Retail and insurance. We provide strategic advice in the areas of search in recruitment process, outsourcing, talent acquisition strategy, recruitment, talent branding and outplacement, and SOW-based recruitment.

Job description:

  • Role :Contributes to all major QA functions to ensure best customer experience with our products
  • Qualification: BE/B.Tech or equivalent degree in Computer Science

Experience/Key Skills

  • Experience:
    • 4 to 8 years in Web based Application & Automation testing.
    • Hands-on experience with tools like Post Man & Rest Assured API Automation Testing.
    • Experience in development of test scenarios for different test phases of client-server applications on Web based & embedded products.
    • Experience on any of the Automation tools and frameworks like Selenium WebDriver using Java & TestNG.
    • Hands on programming knowledge on – Java, Java Scripting & Shell Scripting.
    • Experience in testing backend with Proxy-servers & Load balance testing.
  • Added Advantage :
    • Experience on testing Web based streaming applications such as OTT Domain preferred & Adaptive streaming knowledge.
    • Exposure to any network proxy tool like Charles, Wireshark, and fiddler preferred & having good Networking knowledge.
